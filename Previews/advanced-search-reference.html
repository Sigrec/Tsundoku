Advanced Search

Filter your collection with precise queries. Combine conditions with AND / OR logic.

Syntax

Filter==Value // single filter
Filter==Value & Filter>=Value // AND — both must match
Filter==Value | Filter==Value // OR — either can match
A==x & B==y | C==z // mix — evaluated left to right
